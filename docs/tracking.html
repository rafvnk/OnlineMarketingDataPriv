Preventing Tracking

March 22, 2021 by Rafael Von-Klaveren

Completely avoiding tracking is impossible. Couple of things to reduce the information is to clean your internet browsing history and cookies, and use a privacy browser. Pay attention app permission requests. Check app permissions regularly and one last tip. Stop and think before enabling others to grab your data.

As long as you maintain an online presence. Avoiding tracking 100% is not possible. There isn't much we can do about the data being accessed by these big companies, but you could always use the steps I stated above to prevent it the best way you can.
