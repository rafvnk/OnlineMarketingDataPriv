Edward Snowden is an infamous whistleblower that ended up exposing the CIA on it’s tactics on how they were spying on US citizens to gather as much information as they could. The NSA and the intelligence community is focused on getting intel by any means necessary to serve the national interest. Edward Snowden said it’s tailored in a way that just favors the government agencies in accessing and storing the actual communication from anyone. The authority is given to system administrators or of the same title to wiretap anyone without any given cause, Mr. Snowden said this was against democracy primarily because the government was benefiting privately off the citizens.

Edward Snowden’s main purpose was to allow the public not the employees of the CIA and NSA to allow which information they are enabled to. You’re being watched and recorded regardless, the storage of these facilities increase every year as well. Even if you are accused of something from a wrong call that can enable the CIA and NSA to go back and trace on all the information they have gathered on you beforehand and investigate it.

The Product is You

March 22, 2021 by Rafael Von-Klaveren

Our personal and private experiences are being hijacked by silicon valley and being used as the raw material for extremely profitable digital projects. Every single dose of personal information these companies have is what we actually gave them. Most of the companies argument within tech is that they use data to better their services for us the users, but what they don’t say is a lot of the data in which they absorb are being analyzed to generate models that learn from the patterns of that data which allows them to predict our next move essentially. This applies to every online aspect.

Let’s say if you’re someone who keeps a very low profile online. You rarely use social media, engage in online communities, or keep your online usage very low. If someone has access to only your name they’ll still be able to grab data that is made public online. Birthday, home address, cars you own, emails you’ve used in the past, literally everything. One breadcrumb is all people need to essentially obtain this info that can lead to your physical presence.
